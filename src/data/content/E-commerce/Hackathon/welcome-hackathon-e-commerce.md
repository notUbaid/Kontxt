# You're Building an E-Commerce Store. Here's How to Win.

This isn't a lecture series. It's a build sequence.

Every module in this curriculum is designed to move your project forward by one meaningful step — from zero to a shippable, demo-ready e-commerce store.

---

## What You're Actually Building

By the end of this track, you will have:

- A fully functional online store with real products and checkout
- A polished UI judges can click through
- A working payment flow (or a convincing demo of one)
- A pitch deck and demo script ready for submission

---

## How Hackathon Mode Works

> This track is tuned for speed, polish, and winning — not production readiness.

Every recommendation is filtered through one question:

**Will this matter to a judge in a 3-minute demo?**

If yes → it's here.
If no → it's cut.

You won't be taught things that are correct but irrelevant at your stage. You'll be taught what experienced engineers actually prioritise when building under pressure.

## How to Use This Platform

**Each module gives you:**
- The key decision to make
- How professionals think about it
- Prompts you can use with AI — immediately
- A checklist to validate your output

**Your job:**
- Read actively, not passively
- Make the decision in each module — don't defer it
- Use the prompts, then verify what AI gives you
- Move fast. Hackathons reward momentum.
